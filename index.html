Our Mission

Saving Lives Through Education and Prevention

Global Snakebite Initiative (GSI) is dedicated to reducing snakebite incidents and fatalities in India through comprehensive education and prevention programs. We work directly with communities to provide life-saving knowledge and resources.

Our Impact

Through our initiatives, we've reached thousands of vulnerable communities across India, establishing early warning systems, training local healthcare workers, and implementing preventive measures that have demonstrably reduced snakebite incidents.
